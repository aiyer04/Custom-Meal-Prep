Customized Meal Prep App (Macros-Based)

A personalized meal planning tool that generates weekly meal plans based on user calorie, protein, and fiber goals — helping users stay consistent and achieve their nutrition targets.

Product Overview

The Customized Meal Prep App simplifies nutrition planning by automatically generating meal plans tailored to individual macro goals. It reduces decision fatigue, saves time, and helps users maintain consistency in meeting their daily nutrition needs.

Project Team

Solo PM

  • User research & competitive analysis
  • MVP design & prototype
  • Metrics & A/B testing planning
  • Roadmap and iteration

Problem Space

Users who aim to meet specific nutrition goals often struggle to plan and prepare balanced meals. Manual planning takes time, and existing apps are either too rigid or require extensive input.

  • Difficulty balancing macros (protein, carbs, fats, fiber)
  • Time-intensive meal prep process
  • Lack of actionable, personalized tools

User Research & Insights

Hypothesis: Automatically generated meal plans improve adherence to nutrition goals and increase user satisfaction.

Methods:

  • Surveys and interviews with students and fitness enthusiasts
  • Competitor analysis of MyFitnessPal, Eat This Much, and Mealime

Findings:

  • Existing apps require manual meal entry or lack macro customization
  • Users prefer fast, actionable plans that include grocery lists

Market & Competitive Analysis

Competitor Strengths Weaknesses Opportunity
MyFitnessPal Large food database, strong tracking tools Manual meal creation required Auto-generated plans based on macro targets
Eat This Much Automatic meal plan generation Limited personalization and flexibility Customizable plans with user dietary preferences
Mealime Recipe suggestions, grocery list integration Less focus on nutritional optimization Combine automation with nutrition-focused planning

Solution Concept

Product Vision: Help users meet their nutritional goals effortlessly through automatically generated, personalized meal plans.

MVP Features:

  • Input calorie, protein, and fiber goals
  • Generate weekly meal plan based on macro targets
  • Auto-generate grocery list from selected recipes

Future Roadmap:

  • Integration with local grocery delivery services
  • Budget-based meal suggestions
  • Meal prep tracking and reminder notifications

A/B Testing & Experimentation

Hypothesis: Providing pre-generated meal plans with shopping lists will improve adherence and satisfaction.

  • Control: Meal plan only
  • Variant A: Meal plan + shopping list
  • Variant B: Meal plan + shopping list + meal prep tips

Metrics:

  • Primary: % of meals prepared as planned
  • Secondary: User satisfaction, retention rate, macro adherence

Expected Outcome: Identify the variant that drives the highest adherence and reduces planning friction.

Metrics & Success Criteria

  • Meal plan adherence (% of planned meals completed)
  • Weekly retention and reuse rate
  • User satisfaction survey results
  • Consistency in achieving macro goals

Reflection

  • Identified major challenges in nutrition planning and adherence
  • Designed MVP with macro-driven personalization features
  • Planned structured A/B testing for data-backed improvements
  • Defined success metrics aligned with real-world nutrition outcomes
  • Outlined roadmap for delivery integration and enhanced automation
